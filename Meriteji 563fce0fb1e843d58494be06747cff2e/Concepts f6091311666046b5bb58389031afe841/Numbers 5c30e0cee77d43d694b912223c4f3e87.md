# Numbers

Meriteji has 3 sets of numbers, *dozedel sumbra* (dozenal numbers), *tendel sumbra* (decimal numbers), and *pleitadel sumbra* (“placed” numbers).

## Dozedel Sumbra

Dozedel Sumbra have many irregularities due to sound assimilation.
